5.3.3. Share Group


  1. Click the [Contact] menu from main menu bar on the top of the screen
  1. After clicking the icon beside of selected folder or right clicking of the mouse.
  2. Click the [Share Group] button.



  1. Enter the Email address and select the Role when the[Share Group] popup window is displayed.



Item

Description

Name

Name of the contact

Email

Email address of the contact

Role 

Select a role among 4 types 

None

None

Viewer

View

Manager

View, Edit, Add, Remove

Admin

View, Edit, Add, Remove, Administer


Share Info

Shared info of the contact




5. Click the [Yes] button when a pop up window says ‘ Are you sure you want to share the group?’
