5.1.1. Add Group


  1. Click the [Contact] menu from main menu bar on the top of the screen.
  2. Click the gear icon beside [My Contact] folder or right-click the mouse.
  3. A new pop-up [New Group] button will display and then click on it.
  4. A [Create New Group] dialog is displayed
  5. After fill in the [name]. Click the [Apply] button.



  6. Click the [Yes] button when a pop up window says ‘ Are you sure you want to save the group?’.
