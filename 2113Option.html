2.1.1.3. Option

Setting a signature

User can registering the signature and request read receipt option 

    1. Click the [E-Mail] button on the top menu.
    2. Click the [compose] button.
    3. Click the [option] button at the top 
    4. Choose "Signature" then window will pop-up and there user can fill out the information needed.
    5. Then click [Register] button.


   




 



Add request read receipt 

Users can add request read receipt, which gives a notice when recipients receive and open the email.

    1. Click on the [E-mail] on the top bar.
    2. Click [Compose] button.
    3. Click the [Option] button at the sub top bar. 
    4. Then choose [Request read receipt] button.
