9.0 Introduction to the "E-Approval" component


E-Approval is a process for electronically handling real-world matters by retrieving a document form from the server, drafting an electronic document and sending the draft to the supervisor/manager to request for their approval.


e-Drafting functions are used by workers in their line of duty. Anyone can draft and request approval of documents by using common forms for their organization; such documents go through a workflow of ‘Draft-processing-(Holding)’ before being finally approved and saved.

This folder is for only outgoing managers. All documents already dispatched to external institutions are stored in this folder. Documents in this folder contains only the last signature on the line of approvers (official documents or DDP documents)
