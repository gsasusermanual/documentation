5.1.2. Send E-mail

Users can send E-mail to the selected users directly in contact page


  1. Click the [Contact] menu from main menu bar on the top of the screen
  2. Click a contact you want to send e-mail to and the [New email] button on the top of the screen
  3. Enter the email info and click the [Send] button


Item

Description

Name

Name of the contact
