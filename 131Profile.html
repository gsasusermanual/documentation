1.3.1. Profile

Users can change profile information

  1. Click the user’s icon on the right top of the screen. 
  2. On the dialog shown, Click the [Profile] button on the right toolbar.
  3. Profile is selected.
  4. After updating your profile, click [Save] button to keep the changes.
